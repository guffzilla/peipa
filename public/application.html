To apply for a full practicing membership of the Association:

Must be a graduate of a school recognized by the CFPM. With the exception of new graduates, must provide proof of 20 hours of C.M.E.C.s in the last 24 months. Must successfully complete the NB Podiatry Association licensing exam.
